35% of Bitcoin are believed to be gone forever.

Cryptocrypt helps you set up a delayed message for your loved ones, to help them retrieve your crypto when you're gone.

  • Free

    This service is free of use.
    It's maintained by donations from happy users.

  • Secure

    Data is encrypted in transit, and can be completely deleted at will.
    You can also encrypt your message using the same algorithms that secure blockchains,
    or self-host an instance of cryptocrypt on your own server.

  • Easy

    It only takes a couple minutes to get everything set up.
    And you're then free to live your life.

How does it work?

  1. You create a crypt. You tell us who your next of kin is, and your instructions to help them retrieve your cryptos. (see examples)
  2. You get contacted regularly, and confirm you're still around.
  3. If you fail to answer for some time, we send your message automatically to the intended recipient.
